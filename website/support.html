Frequently Asked Questions

The Walk + Water Challenge is a 3–30 day program inside the APEX AI Coach app. You set a personalized daily step goal and water intake goal, the app tracks your progress, and an AI Coach gives you daily motivation, tips, and answers to your questions about movement, hydration, and nutrition.
Yes — APEX AI Coach is free to download on the App Store and Google Play. The Walk + Water Challenge is included in the free tier. Premium features like unlimited AI coaching conversations and the full APEX fitness program are available via an optional subscription.
The AI Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude, one of the most advanced AI models available. It knows your specific challenge plan — your step goal, water goal, preferred walk time, and primary goal — and gives you personalized answers about walking science, hydration, nutrition timing, and mindset. It's like having a knowledgeable wellness coach available 24/7.
If you started your challenge today and see the wrong day number, try closing and reopening the app. This was a known bug that was fixed in version 1.1. Make sure you're running the latest version by checking the App Store or Google Play for updates.
First, check that you allowed notifications when prompted during the challenge setup. If you missed that, go to your device Settings → APEX AI Coach → Notifications and enable them. Notifications are scheduled for 7:00 AM, 12:30 PM, and 7:00 PM local time each challenge day. If you're still not receiving them, contact support with your device type and OS version.
You can restart the challenge by going to the challenge screen and tapping "Start New Challenge." This will clear your current progress and let you take the quiz again to set new goals. Note: your previous completion record and coach chat history will not be affected.
At the end of your Walk + Water Challenge, you're invited to join a live group workout session led by Coach Josh. This is a Zoom-based session where you'll complete the challenge together with other participants. If you complete the full challenge and join the live session, you unlock access to the full APEX AI fitness program at a special rate.
To delete your account and all associated data, email us at coach@apexai.coach with the subject line "Delete My Account" from the email address linked to your account. We'll process your request within 5 business days. Note: challenge data stored locally on your device (steps, water, plan) will remain until you delete the app.
Subscriptions are managed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) — we don't handle billing directly. To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings: iPhone: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions. Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & Subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before your next renewal date to avoid being charged.
