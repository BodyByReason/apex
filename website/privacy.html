1. Introduction

Welcome to APEX AI Coach ("APEX," "we," "our," or "us"). We operate the APEX AI Coach mobile application and the website at apexai.coach. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using APEX, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app and website.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address (when signing up or joining the waitlist)
  • Quiz answers (daily step habits, water intake, primary goal, preferred walk time, challenge duration)
  • Gender (used only to personalize your walk & water plan)
  • Messages sent to the AI Coach chat
  • Shipping address (collected when you purchase APEX shakes or supplements, used solely to fulfill your order)
  • Zip code (used to surface relevant on-the-go meal and restaurant options near you)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Anonymous usage analytics (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Push notification interaction events

Health & activity data (on-device only):

  • Step counts you manually log or that sync from your device's health app
  • Water intake you log within the app
  • This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your personalized Walk + Water Challenge plan
  • To power AI Coach responses via the Anthropic Claude API
  • To send you push notifications (challenge reminders, motivation, tips)
  • To send waitlist and launch announcement emails
  • To fulfill shake and supplement orders and arrange shipping
  • To show relevant on-the-go meal options based on your zip code
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the product

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. AI Coach & Anthropic

The APEX AI Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI model. When you send a message to the AI Coach, your message is transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate a response.

  • Do not include sensitive personal or medical information in chat messages
  • Anthropic processes messages according to their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy
  • AI responses are for general wellness information only and are not medical advice
  • Your conversation history is stored locally on your device only

5. Data Storage & Security

Most Walk + Water Challenge data (your plan, step logs, water logs, quiz answers) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never synced to our servers.

Account and waitlist data is stored on Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II compliant platform with encryption at rest and in transit. Shipping addresses collected for product orders are transmitted to our fulfillment system and stored securely for order processing purposes only.

We implement industry-standard security practices including HTTPS, encrypted connections, and access controls. No system is completely secure — please keep your device locked and up to date.

6. Data Retention

  • Local device data is retained until you delete the app
  • Waitlist emails are retained until you unsubscribe
  • Account data is retained for the life of your account plus 90 days after deletion
  • Analytics data is retained in anonymized, aggregated form indefinitely

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Disable push notifications via your device's system settings
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

APEX AI Coach is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Third-Party Services

APEX uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — backend infrastructure and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI Coach responses (Claude API)
  • Stripe — payment processing for shake and supplement purchases (Stripe handles all card data; we never store or access your full payment details)
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase history management
  • Expo / EAS — app build and push notification delivery
  • Apple / Google — app distribution and in-app purchases

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where required by law, by sending you an email notification.

Continued use of APEX after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
